== Welcome to the OpenShift Virtualization beginners part

In this first part, you will take your first steps in OpenShift Virtualization.

.Specifically, we will see how to:

* Create custom templates to standardize and accelerate your VM deployments.
* Connect our virtual machines to an enterprise-type VLAN network.
* Set up a UDN network bubble for your VMs' communication.
* Create a robust backup and recovery strategy for your virtual machines by integrating the Veeam Kasten solution.
