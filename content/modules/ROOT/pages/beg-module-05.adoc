= OpenShift Observability

OpenShift natively integrates (at no extra cost) a complete observability stack that collects metrics and can also collect logs (infrastructure, application, and audit).
This entire stack is composed of open-source components that we actively contribute to, such as Prometheus, Thanos, AlertManager, etc.

OpenShift Observability is multi-tenant by default and offers different dashboards based on your role and permissions.

A *cluster admin* will, for example, be able to see dozens of dashboards to have complete visibility of the cluster, a particular project, or a specific application.

An *OpenShift user* will only be able to access metrics and dashboards related to the projects they have access to.
